## Tote Cleaning Procedures

**When to clean:** Totes are visibly dirty

**How to clean:**
1. Remove from use immediately
2. Choose cleaning method:
   - Clean in Place Procedure, OR
   - Three-compartment sink (produce/bakery)
3. Remove all labels and residue
4. Return to service when clean and dry
